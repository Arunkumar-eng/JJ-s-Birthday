Extra! Extra! Jeya Jenilia Celebrates Magical Birthday!

Written by Special Correspondent AK ⚡

The wizarding world rejoices today as the highly esteemed Jeya Jenilia celebrates another year of magic. Sources close to the birthday witch report that festivities are well underway, with celebrations expected to rival a Great Hall feast.

When asked for comment, one close friend stated: "Like Hermione Granger, she's got the brilliance to figure out any spell life throws at her, and the heart to always stand by her friends. She's like my own personal Room of Requirement — always there exactly when I need her."

The Ministry of Magic has reportedly issued a statement hoping her birthday is brighter than Weasley's Wizard Wheezes fireworks, more thrilling than a game of Quidditch, and as warm as a hug from Molly Weasley.

It remains to be seen whether the celebrations will include unauthorized use of flying carpets, though magical law enforcement remains on standby. Regardless, the entire magical community joins in wishing her the happiest of birthdays.
